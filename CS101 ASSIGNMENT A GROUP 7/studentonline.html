Background and History

During the pandemic, online learning took over most students' education. Teaching very quickly switched over from in person teaching to purely online in a short space of a couple of months.
This massively affected the demands for online learning resources and saw the rise in online teaching aids with software such as Microsoft teams seeing a 37% increase in users in just one week during the height of the pandemic. The approach to online teaching differed slightly depending on the school or staff but overwhelmingly 70% of teachers opted for pre-recording lectures and classes.
