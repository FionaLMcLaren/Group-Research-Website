Innovations

Technology such as zoom, or WhatsApp was massively important in keeping a sense of normality for many students. Zoom saw the use of its software increase 30-fold in April 2020 and at its most popular during lockdown saw 300 million users daily. WhatsApp has seen on average a 40% increase in users over the course of the pandemic. With much of online school being asynchronous the ability to keep connected to other students made possible through these technologies had a significant positive impact on the mental health of these students. Even with the boom programs like Zoom and WhatsApp received 54% of students reported a decrease in social interactions.
