Background Information

Covid-19 has changed the technology in primary schools worldwide. The sudden shift from face-to-face to online teaching has had a huge impact on the technology used, as well as students and parents.

Covid-19 has changed the technology in primary schools worldwide.



Virtual Learning Environments

Virtual learning environments played a key role in online education.
Google Classroom, for instance, allowed teachers to upload learning materials for their students.

Teleconferencing Software

Teleconferencing software, such as Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams, was also used. This software allows teachers to communicate with students over a video call. These calls would allow teachers to give feedback, demonstrate and answer questions.
