Background Information

Schools around the world have had to adapt their teaching methods in the wake of the Covid 19 pandemic.
With the global lockdowns in 2020 and 2021, technology had to play an even larger role in delivering learning to students. This has led to a huge change in the technological systems used in delivering students learning.

Historical Background


Traditionally, technology was used sparingly to aid students learning. It was rarely seen as the sole method in delivering course content, but the Covid 19 pandemic changed all of that.
